Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Admission

The entrance is free for all. Guided tours are available for 70 NOK per person, including a printed guide. Group tours can be arranged for 6 or more people.

Food and Drink

There is a café attached to the museum offering light lunches, beverages, and snacks.
